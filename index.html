Projects

I have been working on many projects as components of my degrees or as freelance propjects or just ot learn some technologies and programming languages.

Experimental Evaluation of DSRC/Wi-Fi Hybrid Communication Systems in Intelligent Transportation Systems.

Developing a DSRC / Wi-Fi hybrid communication that can be used in Intelligent Transportation System applications. This is being developed as an alternative low-cost communication system for DSRC and LTE systems that are currently being deployed. Wi-Fi is used for non-essential communications, while DSRC is for essential communications such as approaching emergency vehicles or railway vehicle warnings.

Autonomous Two-Wheeled Robot
self-study

Developing an RoS-based autonomous differential drive robot for indoor navigation using lidar data as a self-study project.

Smart Hydroponic GrowBox

Designed and developed a green house for growing herbs and day-to-day vegetables using the hydropincs. Lighting, humidity, temperature and the liquid flow is being controlled by electronics.

Smart Bottle

Designed a smart water bottle for a Sri Lankan porcelain company. Designed the electronics, the lid housing a solar panel, LED ring light, USB charging and a single button. Ring light is used to notify the user to consume the liquid. Cosumed amount is recorded and can be downloaded to a computer.

Thermal Scanner

Designed and prototyped a wall mount thermal scanner using an infrared thermal imager array and a Raspberry Pi. Included the electronics, code and the enclosure design for the product.

BioStove

Designed an eco friendly stove that utilizes the food wastage to generate biogas and use it to power the stove. The design was a part of a university assignment that included the electronics and the mechanical design.

Autonomous, Two-Wheeled, Self-Balancing Personal Mobility Device

Designed and developed a two-wheeled self-balancing personal mobility platform that can navigate in a predefined indoor environment with obstacles using a lidar and RGBD sensor. Responsible for mechanical design, electronics hardware architecture design and development of an android interface as a GUI for the platform.

IESL Robogames, IESL, Sri Lanka
Runners-up

Designed and developed a robot that travels uneven terrain, following a black line and picking up and unloading a payload. (https://youtu.be/GWvQK7Qm4pw).

SLIIT Robofest, SLIIT, Sri Lanka
2nd Runners-up

Designed and developed a robot that navigates a maze, picks up a payload, senses its colour, follows coloured arrows, and unloads the payload.

Sri Lankan Robotics Challenge, University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka
Finalist

Designed and developed two robots. One of which was controlled using a remote controller to travel on uneven terrain, pick up a box and place it in a hole in the ground to complete the path for the second robot. The second robot could solve a grid maze, picking up and unloading payloads.
